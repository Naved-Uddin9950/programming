Learn More About Our Work And Our Cultural Activities

As the name suggests Madarsa Gulzar Public Middle School is a madarsa which not only focus on modern days education but also our Deeni Taaleem. We conduct a separate class of Deeniyat for each standards, in which we educate our students about our religion and culture.

We also conduct a separate class of Computer Science for students of 5th to 8th standards, in which we educate them about basic computer knowledge, practical uses of computer and it's applications and emerging technologies.
